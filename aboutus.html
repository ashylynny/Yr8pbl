Why create this website?

Yangon is facing increasing traffic congestion, pollution, and transportation challenges that affect the daily lives of commuters. We created Transitfy to encourage people to use smarter and more sustainable transportation choices such as walking, cycling, and public transportation.

OUR PURPOSE

We aim to reduce carbon emitted by private vehicles in Yangon, Myanmar, as much as possible by providing awareness of the current environment status.

Our mission includes advocating the solutions for the development of a sustainable smart city in the transportation system which also benefits the overall public health of our urban residents.

We are ecstatic to be at every step of your journey where you play a part to be involved in this eco-friendly journey for the nation. We hope our users discover astonishing information to contribute for a better future in the next generation.

Transitfy
