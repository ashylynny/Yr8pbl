Cycling

Health Benefits
  • Reduces stress and improves mental health
  • Improves heart health and fitness
  • Helps improve concentration and memory
  • Provides a healthy lifestyle at a low cost

Environmental benefits
  • Reduces traffic jams
  • Supports sustainable and eco-friendly transportation
  • Produces less noise pollution
  • Reduces air pollution and keeps the air cleaner

Walking

Health Benefits
  • Stronger bones and improved balance
  • Reduces risk of obesity and diabetes
  • Boosts mental health by reducing stress and anxiety.
  • Increased muscle strength and endurance

Environmental benefits
  • Walking produces no air pollution
  • Less traffic congestion
  • Lower carbon footprint
  • Reduced noise pollution

Electric Vehicle

Financial Benefits
  • Cheaper than petrol
  • Save 70% of fuel expense
  • No more regular oil changes
  • Reduce needed maintainance

Environmental benefits
  • Doesn't emit harmful greenhouse gases
  • Prevent toxic runoff and hazardous waste
  • Cleaner air and lungs
  • Noise reduction
