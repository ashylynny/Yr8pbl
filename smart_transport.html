What are the impacts of these systems?

  • Electrifying transportation vehicles reduce carbon emssions. contributing to a more healthy environment and also decreases noise pollution.
  • Car-free zones promote walking, cycling and increase green spaces which not only improves public health but clean the atmosphere.
  • Cycling infrastructure enchances safety for cyclists, improves the overall public health, lowers carbon emissions and reduce frequent traffic congestions.
  • Enhanced bus lanes provides dedicated lanes for public transport buses which shows reliability among citizens by cutting down occasional traffic and decreasing time taken when traveling.
  • Road pricing cuts down huge congestions and effectively increases public safety for pedestrians and cyclists as there's a fine for accidents, too.
