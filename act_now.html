Take Action

Every small decision you make today can reduce traffic, pollution, and energy waste. Together, these actions help build a smarter and more sustainable Yangon by 2040.

Walk Short Distances

Walking instead of taking a vehicle for short trips reduces traffic congestion, lowers carbon emissions, and improves personal health and fitness.

Use Public Transport

Public transport helps move more people efficiently, reduces fuel usage, and significantly decreases road congestion in busy city areas.

Carpool with Others

Sharing rides reduces the number of vehicles on the road, saves transportation costs, and helps reduce air pollution levels.

Cycle for Travel

Cycling is a zero-emission transport option that reduces pollution, improves health, and is perfect for short-distance travel.

Support Electric Vehicles

Electric vehicles reduce dependency on fossil fuels and help create a cleaner and more modern transport system for the future.

Spread Awareness

Encouraging others to adopt sustainable transport habits creates a stronger environmental impact across the whole community.

Why It Matters

These actions may look small, but together they create long-term change for the city.

Less Traffic Congestion

Fewer private vehicles lead to smoother traffic flow and faster daily travel.

Cleaner Air Quality

Reduced emissions from vehicles improve air quality and public health.

Lower Transport Cost

Using shared and active transport reduces daily expenses significantly.

Healthier Lifestyle

Walking and cycling increase physical activity and reduce stress levels.

Smarter City Growth

Sustainable transport supports better urban planning and city efficiency.

Future Yangon (2040)

A cleaner, smarter, and more efficient city with improved quality of life.
